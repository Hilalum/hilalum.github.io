Room Board

A third-party Home Assistant dashboard for Apple TV, Google TV, Android TV, iPhone, and iPad.

Room Board displays your Home Assistant entities as room-aware tiles on the big screen. Authentication uses Home Assistant's standard OAuth flow — credentials are entered directly on your own Home Assistant server and never reach Room Board's developer.
