# Privacy Policy for Robot72

**Last Updated: April 27, 2026**

## Introduction

Thank you for using **Robot72** ("the App"). Robot72 is an iOS application for encoding and decoding SSTV (Slow-Scan Television) signals, allowing users to transmit and receive images via audio.

We value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection and Use

**We explicitly state: This App does not collect, store, or share any of your personal information with any third parties.**

1. **No Data Collection**: The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All image processing and audio encoding/decoding happens entirely on your device.

2. **Microphone Access**: The App requests access to your device's microphone solely for the purpose of receiving and decoding SSTV audio signals. Audio is processed in real-time on your device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server.

3. **Photo Library Access**: The App may request access to your photo library only when you choose to save decoded images or select images for transmission. This access is entirely under your control.

4. **Local Storage**: All your data — including decoded images, transmission history, and settings — is stored locally on your device. No data is sent to any external server.

5. **No Backend Services**: The App does not have a backend server maintained by the developer. We cannot access your images, audio, or any other data.

## In-App Purchases

Robot72 offers a one-time in-app purchase ("Robot72 Pro") to unlock additional features. Payment is processed securely through Apple's App Store via your Apple ID.

* We do not have access to your full payment information. Apple provides us with limited transaction data (such as purchase confirmation) necessary to deliver the purchased features.
* Family Sharing is supported and managed by Apple.

## Third-Party Services

* **Apple App Store**: In-app purchases are processed by Apple. For Apple's privacy practices, please refer to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## Data Security

* All data is processed and stored locally on your device.
* The App uses Apple's secure storage mechanisms (e.g., Keychain) where appropriate.
* No data is transmitted over the internet by this App.

## Children's Privacy

This App does not knowingly collect information from children. Since we do not collect any personal data at all, our App is safe for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
